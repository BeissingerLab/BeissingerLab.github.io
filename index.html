Welcome to the Beissinger Lab. We are interested in plant quantitative and population genetic methods and applications. Our research is focussed on the maize (corn) genome. Our primary interests fall into two categories: 1) We are interested in understanding how plants respond to evolutionary forces such as artificial selection, and 2) We are working to achieve an improved understanding of complexities in the genotype-phenotype relationship such as interactions and non-additivity. We are supported by the USDA-ARS at the University of Missouri, Columbia.
